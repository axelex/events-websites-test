WHY PARTICIPATE?

Some key themes for the conference include:

  • Data protection and privacy
  • IT and IoT
  • Compliance and risk management
  • 5G security
  • DevSecOps
  • Identity and access management
  • Cryptocurrency

ISMG's events provide education and exclusive networking opportunities with peers and subject matter experts. CPE credits are also available to all the summit attendees.
